Workshop Organisation

I co-organise the Workshop on Gaze and Eye Movement in Interaction in XR (GEMINI), bringing together researchers and practitioners to discuss gaze-based interaction, 3D user interfaces, and gaze analysis in extended reality environments. The workshop has been held consecutively at IEEE VR 2024, 2025, and 2026. More details can be found on the GEMINI workshop website.

Program Committee

  • Associate Chair, Blending Interaction Committee — CHI 2026
  • Associate Editor, Program Committee, ETRA Short Papers — ETRA 2024
  • Program Committee Member, Workshop on Advancing Physiological Methods in Human-Information Interaction (APhyMeHII) — UbiComp/ISWC 2024

Reviewing

I regularly review for leading venues in human-computer interaction, including ACM CHI, ETRA, UIST, ACM TOCHI, and IMWUT. Additional reviewing includes DIS, ICMI, MobileHCI, OzCHI, IEEE Transactions on Education, and the International Journal of Human-Computer Studies.

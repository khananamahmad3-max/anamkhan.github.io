Workshop Organisation

I co-organise the Workshop on Gaze and Eye Movement in Interaction in XR (GEMINI) at IEEE VR, bringing together researchers and practitioners to discuss gaze-based interaction, 3D user interfaces, and gaze analysis in extended reality environments. The workshop has run consecutively at IEEE VR 2024, 2025, and 2026.

Reviewing

I regularly review for leading venues in human-computer interaction, including ACM CHI (2022–2024), ETRA (2023–2024), ACM TOCHI (2025), and IMWUT (2020, 2021, 2024). Additional reviewing includes DIS, ICMI, MobileHCI, OzCHI, IEEE Transactions on Education, and the International Journal of Human-Computer Studies.
